# ✈️ Asia Air Traffic & Tourism Trends — Python EDA

An exploratory data analysis project examining 20+ years of 
air passenger traffic and tourism trends across Asia using 
two real-world datasets.

## Project Objectives
1. Year-wise air passenger traffic trends in Asia
2. Tourism trends and YoY growth analysis
3. Relationship between air travel and tourism earnings
4. Impact of GDP, inflation and unemployment on tourism
5. Recovery analysis after COVID-19 collapse
6. Statistical summary of key travel indicators
7. Country-wise comparison of tourism performance

## Key Findings
- 2020 caused the sharpest collapse in Asian air travel ever recorded
- 2022 saw 192% passenger rebound — strongest single year growth
- Strong positive correlation between air passengers and tourism receipts
- Higher inflation directly reduces tourism spending
- Air travel recovered faster than tourism arrivals post-COVID
- China, Japan and UAE are Asia's top tourism earners

## Tools & Technologies
- Python — Core analysis
- Pandas & NumPy — Data manipulation
- Matplotlib & Seaborn — Visualizations

## Datasets
- Air Traffic Passenger Statistics
- World Tourism Economy Data
